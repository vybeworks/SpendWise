How it works

Three minutes of setup.
A lifetime of clarity.

No spreadsheets. No manual categorization. Just tell SpendWise your income and fixed costs — it handles the rest every single day.

01
Enter income and fixed costs
Rent, subscriptions, loan payments. Enter them once. SpendWise subtracts them automatically every month.
02
Log what you spend, day by day
Tap Add Expense, enter the amount. Your daily safe-to-spend number updates immediately.
03
Let the number guide you
Safe, Caution, High Risk, or Zero Spend Day. Open the app, see the verdict, make better decisions.
